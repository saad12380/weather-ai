Our Journey

From a research project to a global weather intelligence platform

2020 - Research Begins

Started as a research initiative at Stanford University, exploring machine learning applications in meteorology and climate science.

2021 - First Prototype

Developed the first anomaly detection algorithm achieving 85% accuracy on historical weather data, laying the foundation for our platform.

2022 - Platform Launch

Officially launched Weather AI platform with real-time monitoring for 50+ cities worldwide, serving our first enterprise clients.

2023 - Global Expansion

Expanded to 150+ cities, integrated with major weather data sources, and reached 94.5% detection accuracy with our advanced AI models.

2024 - Enterprise Solutions

Launched comprehensive enterprise solutions serving governments, research institutions, and Fortune 500 companies globally.
