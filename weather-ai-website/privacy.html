Important Notice

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of Weather AI services, including our website, mobile applications, and API services. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

Data Minimization

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To deliver and maintain our weather intelligence platform
  • Communication: To send important updates, notifications, and support responses
  • Improvement: To enhance our services, develop new features, and conduct research
  • Security: To protect our services, users, and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Compliance: To meet legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and optimize user experience
Analytics & Research

We use anonymized and aggregated data for research purposes to improve weather prediction algorithms and climate models. This data cannot be traced back to individual users.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party providers for:

  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Payment processing (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Email communication (SendGrid, Mailchimp)
  • Analytics and monitoring (Google Analytics, New Relic)
Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Court orders or legal processes
  • Government requests
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To prevent fraud or security issues
No Data Selling

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share data with service providers who help us deliver our services, and they are contractually obligated to protect your information.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

Encryption

End-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest

Secure Infrastructure

Enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure with regular security audits

Access Controls

Role-based access controls and multi-factor authentication

Regular Audits

Continuous security monitoring and regular penetration testing

Security Standards

We adhere to industry-standard security frameworks including ISO 27001, SOC 2, and implement GDPR-compliant data protection measures.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access & Portability

Request access to your personal data and receive it in a structured, commonly used format.

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.

Restriction

Request restriction of processing of your personal data.

Objection

Object to processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes.

Withdraw Consent

Withdraw your consent at any time where we rely on consent to process your data.

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@weatherai.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.
